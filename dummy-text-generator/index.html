How to use the dummy text generator

What is lorem ipsum?

Lorem ipsum is scrambled Latin-derived placeholder text used by designers since the 1500s to fill layouts without distracting readers with real content. Our generator produces authentic lorem ipsum text instantly.

How to generate lorem ipsum

Select Lorem ipsum tab, choose your output type (paragraphs, sentences, words, or bytes), set your count, and click Generate. Toggle HTML tags or ALL CAPS as needed, then copy with one click.

What is the website content generator?

Switch to the Website content tab to generate realistic placeholder copy for every page of your site — hero sections, product descriptions, team bios, FAQ items, pricing plans, menu items, and more.

Which website types are supported?

Seven types are built in: Ecommerce, Blog / Magazine, SaaS / Software, Portfolio, Restaurant, Agency / Studio, and Nonprofit / NGO — each with its own set of pages and section-specific content.

How do I customise the generated content?

Enter your brand name, topic or niche, target audience, and a brief description. The generator uses this context to produce headlines, product names, CTAs, and body text that match your actual website.

Yes — completely free, forever. No account, no sign-up, no watermarks. Built by Ajay K. at Digital BigHit for designers and developers who need fast, clean placeholder content.
